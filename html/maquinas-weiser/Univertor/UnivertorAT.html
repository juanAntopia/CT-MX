Univertor AT

Univertor

Modular and compact twin-spindle pick-up lathe for highly productive synchronous processing of two workpieces with identical process steps.

  • Compact installation surface with maximal output
    Workpiece diameters of up to 125 mm
    2-axis correction slide for high and adjustable accuracy on both workpieces
    Two precision turning spindles for maximum accuracy and process stability

The powerful twin-spindle machine (one work area, two working spindles) with the original WEISSER pick-up system is able to synchronously machine two workpieces with identical process steps according to the twin principle.

With the same installation area and lower investment costs, the productivity can be doubled. This makes the UNIVERTOR AT ideally suited for use in high-volume production. The simultaneous complete processing of twice the number of workpieces within the same time leads to a reduction of processing times of nearly 50%.

Modular single-spindle lathe with vertical spindle-slide layout. The UNIVERTOR A machine models are designed to be flexible and are ideally suited for cost-effective complete processing of various batch sizes while meeting high demands in terms of cutting technology and quality. Right or left machine configuration versions as well as spindles varying in power allow ideal adaptation to the production flow.
